Autonomous Claw

Autonomous Claw

The Autonomous Claw is an Arduino controlled, sheet metal claw which used a servo to control the open/close functions. The claw is designed to be able to detect and pick up objects of various sizes and at the same time pick up bulk amounts of smaller objects, such as dried pasta shells.

Future Improvements:

The claw was a group project that ended up being rushed to complete. This caused the claw to not be aligned and sturdy, thus reducing the reliability of it's performance. A cleaner construction would have led to a better performance.
